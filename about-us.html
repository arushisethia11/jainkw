About Us

Jain Knit Wears was set up in year 1974 by Shri Amar Chand Sethia at Pahri Dhiraj,Sadar bazaar,Delhi . In year 2003, we shifted our factory to Gandhinagar, Delhi. Jain Knit Wears has been now in existence for more than 37 years, having facilities for manufacturing all kinds of readymade garments and in-house facility of garment designing. We deal in the Delhi and outside Delhi region also.

Jain Knit Wears is one of the well established manufacturer and supplier of school uniform & accessories, unisex jackets, all kind of tshirts, track suits, lowers, bermudas and much more. The company is backed by the team of highly skilled workforce that have an expertise in making of top class products as per the requirements of diverse industries using imported machines of the highest grade. We make use of high-grade material which is well tested by the team of quality controllers before its usage during the production process. All kinds of raw material are being sourced from the trusted vendors of the market that ensures the long term durability of the products.

List of products that we produce:

  • School Uniform & Accessories (includes school uniform, socks, ties, belts, badges and more...)
  • Tshirts (all kind of tshirts - polo tshirts, printed tshirts, tshirts for promotions and events, more...)
  • Jackets (unisex and men's jackets, sweat shirts and more...)
  • Track Suits (includes bermudas, lowers and more...)
